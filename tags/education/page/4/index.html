I Believe CS Teachers are Smart and Capable

People sometimes accuse me of being an elitist CS snob that I feel that CS teachers have to have advanced CS degrees. It's not that at all. Rather, I feel that CS teachers are smart and capable. Circumstances frequently result in a teacher in front of a CS class with limited content and pedagogical content knowledge. It's not ideal but it's reality. Of course I feel that we should do everything to support and encourage these teachers but we should also insist that after a reasonable period of time, they know their stuff.
